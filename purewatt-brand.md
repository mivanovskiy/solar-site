## Company Overview

**Company name:** Purewatt
**Industry:** Solar energy brokerage and consulting
**Location:** Florida (serves all of Florida)
**Website purpose:** Lead generation — get homeowners to request a free savings analysis

### What Purewatt Is
Purewatt is a solar brokerage and energy consulting company. We do not install solar systems ourselves. Instead, we work with a network of vetted installers and program structures to identify the best solution for each homeowner.

Our role is to act as an advisor — not a salesperson tied to a single product. We analyze the market and match each homeowner with the most cost-effective and appropriate solar option available.

### Core Value Proposition
- Immediate reduction in electricity costs (typically 30–40% lower from day one)
- No upfront cost
- No loan required in most cases
- Predictable and controlled energy pricing
- Fully maintained and insured system
- No operational or repair responsibility for the homeowner

### What Makes Purewatt Different
- **Independent advisory model** — not tied to a single installer, product, or financing structure
- **Focus on cost reduction** — we position the solution as lowering energy cost, not selling a product
- **Flexible structures** — multiple pathways including options that avoid loans and ownership responsibilities

### Summary Statement
Purewatt helps homeowners lower their electricity costs by transitioning them to a more efficient and predictable energy solution, while handling the complexity of sourcing, structuring, and implementing the system on their behalf.

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Primary display font:** Barlow Condensed or DM Sans — bold, clean, industrial feel matching the logo
- **Body font:** Inter or DM Sans — highly readable, modern, neutral
- **Fallback:** system-ui, sans-serif

### Type Scale
| Level | Size | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Hero headline | 56–72px | 800 | Main homepage headline |
| Section headline | 36–48px | 700 | Section titles |
| Card title | 20–24px | 600 | Service card headers |
| Body | 16–18px | 400 | Paragraph text |
| Small / caption | 13–14px | 400 | Labels, tags, fine print |

### Typography Rules
- Headlines should be tight and bold — letter-spacing: -0.02em on display sizes
- Body text line-height: 1.7 for readability
- Never center-align body paragraphs — left-align only
- Orange `#FF6B00` can be used to highlight key words in headlines

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Approachable but premium** — warm and easy to understand, but clearly professional
- **Confident, not pushy** — we present facts and savings, not pressure tactics
- **Clear and direct** — no jargon, no filler. Every sentence should earn its place

### Voice Guidelines
- Speak to the homeowner's wallet, not their environmental conscience
- Lead with the financial benefit: lower bills, predictable costs, no risk
- Avoid: "going green," "save the planet," "renewable future" — these are secondary
- Use: "lower your bill," "lock in your rate," "no upfront cost," "we handle everything"
- Write in second person — "you," "your home," "your savings"

### Tagline Options (select one or propose alternatives)
1. **"Lower your bill. Keep your peace of mind."**
2. **"Your energy. Your terms. Lower costs."**
3. **"Pure savings. No guesswork."**
4. **"Power your home for less."**

## Design Direction

### Overall Aesthetic
- Clean, modern, premium but approachable
- White-dominant with bold orange accents
- Generous whitespace — never crowded
- Photography: real Florida homes with solar installations, sunny skies, happy families
- Icons: simple, line-based, consistent stroke weight

### What to Avoid
- Generic solar company green color schemes
- Busy layouts with too many elements competing
- Stock photo-feeling imagery (overly posed, corporate)
- Dark mode as the primary design — this site should feel bright and trustworthy
- Excessive animations that slow the page or distract

### Inspiration References
- **Lumio Solar (lumio.com)** — clean process explanation, approachable tone
- **Tesla Solar** — minimalist layout, premium feel, strong photography
- **SunPower** — interactive elements, savings focus

## Technical Notes for Claude Code

### Stack
- Framework: Astro (App structure)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Hosting: Netlify
- Forms: Netlify Forms
- Images: Cloudinary (optimize all photos and logo files through Cloudinary)
- Animations: Defer until Phase 2 (Framer Motion)
- Video: Defer until Phase 2 (Vimeo)

### Performance Requirements
- Target Lighthouse score: 90+ on all metrics
- Images: lazy load all below-fold images
- Fonts: load via font-display: swap to prevent render blocking
- No JavaScript-heavy components on initial load — Astro islands only where needed

### Logo Implementation
- Store logo files in `/public/images/logos/`
- Use PNG files with transparent backgrounds as provided
- Serve via Cloudinary for optimization
- Include both horizontal and icon versions

### Accessibility
- All images require descriptive alt text
- Color contrast must meet WCAG AA minimum
- Form labels must be properly associated with inputs
- Focus states visible on all interactive elements
